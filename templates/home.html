We know you.

UniReq utilizes a meticulous filtering and ranking system to assist students in finding suitable universities based on their International Baccalaureate (IB) scores. By considering factors such as minimum score requirements, subject prerequisites, language proficiency, and user preferences, the algorithm generates a refined list of universities that align with the student's academic achievements. Additionally, UniReq's personalized ranking mechanism compares the student's IB scores against admission criteria to provide guidance for selecting the best-matched universities.
